Dashboard

The Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of your financial activities.It features important notices to keep you informed, a list of recent transactions for easy tracking, frequently used utilities for quick access, and a dedicated section to manage your credit card payments—all in one convenient view.

Pay Bills

Simplify payments with our intuitive Payment Initiation page. Enter the amount, select your beneficiary,pick from a list of cards, choose the transaction type, and set the settlement date—all designed for a seamless and secure payment experience.

Histories

Keep your financial records organized with the History page. View your transaction history at a glance with clear, detailed information, helping you track and manage your payments efficiently.

Utilities

Explore a wide range of utility services displayed in a visually appealing grid view. Whether it’s electricity,water, or internet bills, the Utilities page makes finding and selecting the right service effortless.

Mobile Recharge

Recharge your accounts effortlessly with our Recharge page. From mobile top-ups to other services, this feature is designed to make payments fast, easy, and hassle-free.

Login & Signup

Experience a combined Login and Signup page for effortless account access. Switch seamlessly between logging in and signing up, ensuring a smooth and user-friendly onboarding process.
